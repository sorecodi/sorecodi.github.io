SO Re Co Di

Spectral and Optimization Techniques for Robust Reconstruction, Combinatorial Constructions, and Distributed Algorithms

This project started in September, 2019, and is supported by the European Research Council. It applies techniques from linear algebra and from convex optimization to problems of robust reconstruction and robust statistics; to the construction of combinatorial objects such as graph and hypergraph sparsifier; and to the analysis of probabilistic network processes.
